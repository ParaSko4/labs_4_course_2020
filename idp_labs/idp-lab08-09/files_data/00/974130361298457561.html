23-Man Ireland Matchday Squad To Play Denmark Named

18 Nov 2019 by PJ Browne

Ireland's matchday squad for Monday night's crucial Euro 2020 qualifier against Denmark has been announced.

Jack Byrne and Troy Parrott, both of whom played in last week's win against New Zealand, have been included.

Callum O'Dowda and Scott Hogan are the two to miss out.

Shane Duffy will tonight captain the team for the first time.

A victory for Ireland will see Mick McCarthy's side qualify for next summer's Euro 2020 finals.

However, if they do not beat Denmark tonight, Ireland can still qualify for the finals via the playoffs which take place at the end of March. A spot in the playoffs was secured over the weekend.

23-man Ireland matchday squad to play Denmark

Goalkeepers

Darren Randolph, Kieran O'Hara, Mark Travers

Defenders

Matt Doherty, Shane Duffy, Kevin Long, John Egan, Ciaran Clark, Enda Stevens

Midfielders

Alan Browne, Josh Cullen, Jeff Hendrick, Glenn Whelan, Conor Hourihane, James McClean, Jack Byrne, Robbie Brady

Forwards

Callum Robinson, Troy Parrott, David McGoldrick, James Collins, Sean Maguire, Alan Judge
