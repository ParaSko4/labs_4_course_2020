Central Statistical Office launches Census 2020 recruitment drive

18 Nov 2019 by Central Statistical Office

(PRESS RELEASE) —  The Central Statistical Office of Saint Lucia wishes to announce the launch of its recruitment drive for the 2020 Population and Housing Census.

This national recruitment effort is expected to hire approximately 500 temporary workers to help conduct the 2020 census. The positions available are Census Mappers and Listers; Census Enumerators; and Census Supervisors.

The application deadline for census mappers and listers is Nov. 30, 2019. The application deadline for census enumerators and supervisors is Jan. 31, 2020.

Application forms can be accessed at

https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/census2020slu

For contact details and application requirements, please view the PDF attached. For further information, visit stats.gov.lc or contact the Central Statistical Office at 758.468.1577.
