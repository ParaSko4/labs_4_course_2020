Oviya posts hot new pics to satisfy fans - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

18 Nov 2019

Oviya was the first celebrity to catch the attention of the audiences in the first season of 'Bigg Boss' hosted by Kamal Haasan and she became the darling of the social media users.  However, she could not capitalize on the huge popularity gained as her films after the show '90 ML' and 'Kalavani 2' failed miserably at the box office and her role in the super hit 'Kanchana 3' was not good enough to give her any mileage in her career.

Oviya has been keeping a low profile for the past few months and one of her fans requested her on social media to at least post pic a week to keep her Ovya Army interested.  As a big surprise Oviya put up her latest pic on Twitter and her fans started sharing it in a big way.

Soon others from the Oviya army started demanding photos from her and the star once again obliged and posted one with a weird hairdo, one with a tattoo and another with her dressed in yellow and blowing a kiss.  That should sure keep the loyal fans happy for some time.
