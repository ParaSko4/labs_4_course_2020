Promoter sells Rs 57 crore worth shares of Jubilant Life Sciences

Promoter entity sold shares at a price of Rs 493 per share, as per bulk deals data available on the BSE website.

18 Nov 2019

Jubilant Stock Holding reduced its shareholding in Jubilant Life Sciences by selling 11.6 lakh equity shares at a price of Rs 493 apiece. The deal totalled roughly Rs 57.19 crore, as per data available on the exchange.

The promoter and promoter group held 50.68 percent stake in Jubilant Life as of September 2019, of which, Jubilant Stock Holding held 14.14 percent.

The stock closed at Rs 494.50, up Rs 2.20, or 0.45 percent on the BSE.
