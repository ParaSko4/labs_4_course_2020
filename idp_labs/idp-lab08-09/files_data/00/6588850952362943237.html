Shatta Wale Jams To Stonebwoy's Hero At 4stye TV Awards 2019

18 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The 2019 4stye TV Music Video Awards came off on Saturday, November 16, 2019, at Kempinksi Executive Lounge in Accra.

The event was put together to award musicians who have done well under the year in review. On the night, many artistes were billed to perform including Ashiaman-born dancehall sensation, Stonebwoy.

When Stonebwoy appeared on the stage, he performed one of his hit songs Hero.

In a video sighted by YEN.com.gh, Shatta Wale who was in the auditorium couldn't control himself as he got up from his seat to jam to Stonebwoy's performance.

When Stonebwoy saw Shatta Wale he got down from `the stage to hug his former rival.

It could be recalled that during Obrafour's Pae Mu Ka @20 Concert Shatta Wale was captured dancing while Samini was performing.

Not only did Shatta Wale dance but he also pulled his photo to take images during the Samini's performance.
