Bono East Regional Minister Enstooled As

18 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Bono East regional minister who also double as Member of Parliament for Atebubu/Amantin Constituency, Hon. Kofi Amoakohene has been enstooled as "bunyanwura Gariba" meaning " Respectful and Submissive leader" at Kamanpa.

He was enstooled during the Celebration of 2019 *Kafojo Kwabena Yam Festival* by the chief's and the people of Kamanpa.

According to the Gonja Chief of Yeji, Yussif Abubakari Mufungi who Spoke on behalf of Kamanpa Chief , Nana Kwame Wande II explained that this is the first time a regional minister has visited their community and for that matter they have to honour him for even accepted their invitation and has fulfilled his promised by joining them to celebrates this years festival.


Afterwards, Bono East regional minister Hon.Kofi Amoakohene also appreciated the chiefs for honouring him with such a great position in their traditional area.

He appealed to the people's at Kamanpa and it's surrounding environs to support the npp government.

He also educated them on the importance of the upcoming referendum and therefore calls them to come out in their numbers and vote YES.
