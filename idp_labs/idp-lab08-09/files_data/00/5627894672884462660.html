Deputy shoots gunman dead at Lake Elsinore

18 Nov 2019

LAKE ELSINORE  (INT)  A man whod been brandishing a gun was shot dead during a physical altercation with a sheriffs deputy.

A man-with-a-gun call brought the officer to an address on Ash Street late Saturday.

A confrontation turned physical and the man was shot, according to a statement from the Riverside County sheriffs department. Paramedics responded until the man died.

No one else was injured.

Neither the mans name nor other details were immediately released.
