Xbox South Africa Black Friday Console Deals Revealed

18 Nov 2019 by Marco Cocomello

If you are shopping around for a console this Black Friday in South Africa and you are looking for an Xbox One then you are in luck. Prima Interactive, the local distributors for Xbox hardware in SA have revealed their three key Black Friday deals that will be running over the course of the week. Starting on 25 November and ending on 2 December 2019, you will be able to pick up a brand new Xbox One S or Xbox One X for a couple of thousands of rands off.

First off we have the Xbox One S 1TB model with one controller for R3,599. You can then get an Xbox One S 1TB with an extra controller for R4,699. Lastly, the Xbox One X is R2,000 at R6,999.

  • Xbox One S 1TB – R3,599
  • Xbox One S + Wireless Controller – R4,699
  • Xbox One X – R6,999

Prima revealed that the deals will be live from 25 November so you don’t want to wait until Black Friday, which is on the 29th in order to take advantage of the sale. Keep in mind that other retailers may also bundle a game with the above for a bit more on the price so shop around for the best deals before taking the dive.

The above sales will be at participating retailers in SA. If they sell Xbox One consoles, it will be on sale during that week at the store.

We will be sharing many more Black Friday South Africa deals as we lead up to the big shopping day so stay tuned for more.
