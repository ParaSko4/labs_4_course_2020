Joel Klatt Releases His New Top 10 After Week 12

18 Nov 2019, 00:56 by Tzvi Machlin

Week 12 of the 2019 college football season has come and gone, but not before bringing us all plenty of chaos to mull over. Tua Tagovailoa is done for the year with a hip injury, while two more undefeated teams – Minnesota and Baylor – lost their first games of the season.

Heading into Week 13, FOX Sports college football analyst Joel Klatt has updated his list of the top 10 teams.

Unsurprisingly, the three remaining undefeated teams take the top spots, but the order may come as a bit of a surprise.

Here is Joel Klatt’s Top 10 teams in college football for Week 13:

10. Penn State
9. Minnesota
8. Oklahoma
7. Alabama
6. Utah
5. Georgia
4. Oregon
3. LSU
2. Clemson
1. Ohio State
