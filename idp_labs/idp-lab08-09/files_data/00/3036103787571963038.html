Big Pick n Pay Black Friday tech specials

18 Nov 2019 by Staff Writer

Pick n Pay has launched its latest Black Friday deals, offering big price cuts on a wide variety of tech products.

Black Friday is a tradition which South Africa adopted from the United States. It takes place on the day after Thanksgiving and signals the start of the holiday shopping season.

Black Friday is the biggest online shopping day in South Africa, and many local retailers are expected to reach record sales this year.

Black Friday 2019 only takes place on Friday 29 November, but Pick n Pay has launched Black November deals with new specials going live every Monday at 07:00.

The retailer is offering discounts of up to 50% across various categories which are valid for seven days or until stocks last.
