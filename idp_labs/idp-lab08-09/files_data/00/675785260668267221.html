I'm A Celeb fans predict fiery rows between Ian and Caitlyn after reality star 'mocks' pundit

There was already an awkward moment between sports pundit Ian Wright and reality star Caitlyn Jenner on I'm A Celebrity

18 Nov 2019, 00:55 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

I'm A Celebrity fans are already predicting that there are fiery rows ahead for Ian Wright and Caitlyn Jenner after noticing a tense moment between the pair.

This evening, ten brand new celebrities entered the jungle, ready to jump into the terrifying tasks awaiting them.

However they were quickly divided up as four celebs became power pairs, meaning they got to stay in the luxury camp.

As a further reward, they got kangaroo to eat for their dinner.

Unfortunately for sports pundit Ian and reality star Caitlyn, they landed in the other camp where they were put on rations of rice and beans.
