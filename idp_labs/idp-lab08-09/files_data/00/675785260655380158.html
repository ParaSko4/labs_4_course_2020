Lottery winner spends £3million jackpot on boob jobs for herself and her sisters

Sarah Cockings, 36, from Whitley Bay, North Tyneside, says people ask her in the street "are those the lottery boobs?" after she used her windfall to fund the plastic surgery

18 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Jeremy Armstrong, Adam Aspinall, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/adam-aspinall/

A £3million lottery winner has treated herself to a boob job after spending almost £10,000 on the same op for her two sisters.

Sarah Cockings, paid for plastic surgery for Emma, 37, and Alex, 32, as well as splashing out on a house for her parents, a Mini Cooper and a Range Rover Sport.

She also went back to university because her mum “wanted that picture of me graduating – which she has now on her mantelpiece”.

Sarah, 36, once joked how people stopped her sisters in the street to ask:
'Are those the lottery boobs?'

Celebrating the lottery's 25th birthday with other winners, Sarah, of Whitley Bay, North Tyneside, said: "Just after I won, I bought my two sisters boob jobs; everyone remembers that.

"But now – I’ve had one myself, because mine were wrecked by breastfeeding. It’s my two-year boob birthday. I love them!

 

"My eldest son is six, and he tells everyone I won the Lottery.

"He came home from school and said ‘I told my teacher you won the jackpot and she was laughing'.

"I bought my house outright, and I have a nice car but I live a very normal life."

She went back to university after winning, because her mum 'wanted that picture of me graduating – which she has now on her mantelpiece'.

Sarah, a mum-of-three, added: "My sisters and I live just seven minutes from each other, and our children are all best friends."
