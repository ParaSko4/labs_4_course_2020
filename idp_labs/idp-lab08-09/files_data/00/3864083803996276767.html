2019 Spartan Para Elite World Team Championship Results

18 Nov 2019 by Bonnie Wilson

The 2019 Spartan Para Elite World Championships took place last weekend in Laughlin, Nevada.  Ten teams took to the course for an inspiring competition. Team Straight Outta Legs took first place with a time of 1:14:20, followed closely by Never Stumped International with 1:26:25, and Still Loading with 1:29:30.
