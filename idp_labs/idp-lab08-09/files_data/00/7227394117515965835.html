Realme 5 arrives in Europe on November 21, in time for Black Friday

18 Nov 2019 by Ricky

Realme’s recent entry into European markets debuted with the Realme X2 Pro last month, along with the Realme 5 and Realme 5 Pro. Today, Realme announces that the Realme 5 will become available in European markets starting on November 21, just in time to make sales this upcoming Black Friday (November 29).

The Realme 5 is an entry-level quad-camera smartphone. The handset uses a 6.5-inch LCD display with 720p resolution protected by Gorilla Glass 3+. Driving that large display is Android 9 Pie with Color OS layered on top and powered by the Snapdragon 665AIE paired with 3GB or 4GB of RAM – it’s not clear yet which RAM/Storage setup will come to Europe.

You can expect insane battery endurance thanks to the 5,000 mAh battery – it ranked top 4 amongst all the phones we’ve tested for battery endurance. Meanwhile, the four-camera setup consists of a 12MP main camera, 8MP ultrawide, 2MP macro, and a 2MP depth sensor. There’s also a 13MP selfie camera.

The Realme 5 will be available in Crystal Blue and Crystal Purple starting on November 21. Realme will announce availability and pricing throughout Europe on November 21, along with specifications – particularly the memory configuration.
