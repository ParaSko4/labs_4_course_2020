Robert Moreno cancels press conference after beating Romania

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Spain national team coach, Robert Moreno, decided to cancel the press conference after the game against Romania so that he did not have to speak about his future.

Spain won by five goals to nil and head into the Euro 2020 competition undefeated, but before the game against Romania, news came out that Robert Moreno was not going to continue as Spain coach for the Euro's next year.

Once the game had finished, and with the win under his belt and the press conference room full, Robert Moreno decided to not give a press conference in order to avoid having to explain what is happening in the near future with the Spanish national team and its coach.

This took everybody by surprise, and maybe suggests that his possible departure from the Spanish national team is more real and has only increased the rumours about Luis Enrique's possible return.
