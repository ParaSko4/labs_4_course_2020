UK Youth Wing Storms Cardiff, Wales, On Government Policy Sensitisation

18 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

NPP UK Youth Wing leadership under the hard working leadership of Richard Gyamfi popularly known as Youth Jesus , visited Tertiary Education Students Confederacy Of NPP (TESCON) in Cardiff, Wales under the Youth Wing University Visitation Project.

The leadership encouraged the students to continue to push forward the agenda of the Danquah-Busia-Dombo tradition, now led by President Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo, wherever they find themselves, as it is the only tradition that believes in the emancipation of the Ghanaian youth via education and entrepreneurship.

The students led by Robert Bosco, discussed with leadership their vision to reach out to all Ghanaian students in Wales and also expressed several challenges they faced. They were quick to add that, the NPP UK Youth leadership’s visitation has further strengthened their commitment to NPP irrespective of where they find themselves.

Richard Gyamfi, Branch Youth Organiser, praised the team for being part of history as this was the first time an NPP UK wing has met in Wales, officially. He cautioned them to value their little beginning and said the NPP UK Branch, under the able leadership of Derek Kwaku Nkansah , will support them in their sacrosanct quest to multiply their numbers in the near future.
The team took the opportunity to visit few a Ghanaian shops in Cardiff to discuss the many policies currently being rolled out in Ghana by the NPP administration. The team was made up of Eunice Acheampomaa (Branch Dep YO), Thomas Oheneba Boakye (Secretary, Youth Wing) and King Acheampong ( Treasurer - Swindon Chapter).
