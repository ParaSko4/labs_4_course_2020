Emily Ratjakowski Poses With Husband Sebastian Bear McClard In Sweet Selfie Silencing Rumors The Couple Was Breaking Up

18 Nov 2019, 00:40 by Charisse Van Horn

Emily Ratjkowski usually posts pictures of herself modeling her Inamorata Woman clothes on her Instagram accounts but in one of her recent photos, she chose to share an adorable picture of herself with her husband Sebastian Bear McClard. The picture comes as rumors circulated online that Emily and Sebastian may have been having marriage problems. The origins of the rumors seemed to have begun when Emily posted some pictures on Instagram then deleted them. Though it’s impossible to tell what is really going on in a person’s life based on the pictures they post on social media, many fans were concerned that Emily deleted the pictures because there was trouble between herself and Sebastian.

Not only did Emily share a photo of Sebastian and herself snuggling up against each other, but they were also wearing matching sweatshirts. If Emily and Sebastian wanted to send a message to the naysayers that they were still a couple in love, they succeeded.

Emily and Sebastian will celebrate their second-year anniversary in February 2020. They surprised everyone with a courthouse wedding and many people questioned why they chose to get married so quickly.

So far, it seems there aren’t any obvious problems in Sebastian’s and Emily’s marriage and the couple seems very much in love when they are seen together out in public.

Emily and Sebastian made a public appearance at Kendall Jenner’s Halloween/Birthday party and the couple often travels between New York and Los Angeles.

You may see the adorable photo of Emily and Sebastian below.

Fans love to see photos of Sebastian and Emily together and the couple are often spotted walking their rescue-dog Colombo. There’s no doubt that fans would love to see more pics of Sebastian and Emily together even though her racier pictures tend to get the most likes. Emily’s fans are happy for her now that she is married and they want to see more of how she and Sebastian interact at home.
