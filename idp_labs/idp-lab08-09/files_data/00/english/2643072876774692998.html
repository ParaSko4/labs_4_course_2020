Gov. Oyetola Congratulates Yahaya Bello On His Re-Election

18 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

The Osun State Governor, Gboyega Oyetola, has congratulated the candidate of the All Progressives Congress in Kogi state, Yahaya Bello, oh his re-election.

Naija News had reported that the Independent National Electoral Commission, declared Yahaya Bello as the winner of the November 16 election.

The Kogi State governor was declared as the winner having scored 406, 222 votes to defeat his closest rival Musa Wada of the Peoples Democratic Party, PDP, who 189, 704.

Oyetola while congratulating Bello on his re-election also commended President Muhammadu Buhari and APC.

The Osun State governor who took to his Twitter account wrote, “Congratulations to Kogi State Governor-elect, Yahaya Bello, over his re-election at the poll.”

“Congratulations to President Muhammadu Buhari and the entire APC family for this victory. To God be the glory. APC!”
