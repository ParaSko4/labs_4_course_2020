Sai Pallavi, Rashmika in the same zone - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

18 Nov 2019

If there is anything that is similar between Sai Pallavi and Rashmika Mandanna, it has to be how they have surprised themselves by defying the norms in the glamour-driven entertainment industry.  'Premam' (Malayalam) in the case of the 'Fidaa' actress and 'Kirik Party' (Kannada) in the case of the 'Geetha Govindam' actress proved to be a boon.  

Sai Pallavi had been under the impression that her pimples would be act as a dampener in films.  "I had a lot of pimples in 'Premam', but people accepted me as I was, and I learnt that confidence is the real beauty," the talented woman, who is a qualified doctor, once said.  

Rashmika, like Pallavi, hasn't really relied on looks.  Instead, she has been a confident actress.  Since her first film, the 'Sarileru Neekevvaru' actress has focused on the story of the film rather than giving importance to glamour.
