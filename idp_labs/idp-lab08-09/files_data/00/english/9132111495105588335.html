NDC opens parliamentary nomination in 20 constituency in the Ashanti Region

18 Nov 2019

The main opposition party National Democratic Congress (NDC) has opened nominations in the Ashaiman Constituency for prospective aspirants to contest the forthcoming parliamentary primaries on Monday 18th November.

The aspirants are expected to pick their nomination forms from Monday November 18, 2019 to Wednesday November 20, 2019 in accordance with Article 41(1) of the NDC Constitution.

The constituencies are Atwima Nwabiagya North, Atwima Nwabiagya South, Afigya Kwabre North, Kwabre East, Obuasi East, Obuasi West, Adansi Akrofuom, Manso Adubia, Bekwai, Nsuta Kwaman Beposo, Afigya Sekyere East, Kumawu, Effiduase Asokore, Bosome Freho, Asante Akim South, Asante Akim North, Asante Akim Central, Ejisu, Oforikrom and Bantama.

Below is the full statement

NDC opens parliamentary nomination in 20 constituency in the Ashanti Region

The Functional Executive Committee in Consultation with the Ashanti Regional Executive Committee of the National Democratic Congress announces the notice of elections for the conduct of Parliamentary Primary Elections in 20 constituencies in the Ashanti Region in accordance with Article 41(1) of the NDC Constitution.

Nominations will open for 3 days from Monday November 18, 2019 to Wednesday November 20, 2019. The following dates shall also apply for the other electoral activities:

Filing of nomination: November 25, 2019

Vetting of Aspirants: 26th and 27th November, 2019

Election: December 14, 2019

The Constituencies are: Atwima Nwabiagya North, Atwima Nwabiagya South, Afigya Kwabre North, Kwabre East, Obuasi East, Obuasi West, Adansi Akrofuom, Manso Adubia, Bekwai, Nsuta Kwaman Beposo, Afigya Sekyere East, Kumawu, Effiduase Asokore, Bosome Freho, Asante Akim South, Asante Akim North, Asante Akim Central, Ejisu, Oforikrom and Bantama

All stakeholders and interested persons should take note and comply accordingly.
