The Internet Is Afraid Of This Definitely Possessed Baby And You Should Be Too

18 Nov 2019, 00:49 by Michelle Rennex

Everyone on Twitter is scared of this demonic baby, and look, it’s justified.

Whether you’re a horror movie buff or not, you’ll likely known the signs of a demonic possession. You know, uncontrolled convulsions, obvious voice changes and distinct personalities, upside-down crosses, and well, glowing, soulless eyes.

Twitter user @PassionPopSoc experienced this the hard way. She decided to purchase a new baby monitor so she could see her son while he slept. Unfortunately for her, either her son or the monitor is 110% absolutely, undoubtedly possessed.
