The visit to the Metropolitano that surprised Ramos and Morata

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Spain will face Romania on Monday in the 2nd match of the international break. The match will be played at the Metropolitano, the stadium where Ramos and Morata received a beautiful surprise.

There are times that we all need a nudge, and that is what happened to the CB and striker.

Spain trained in the morning in a quieter session under Robert Moreno, but at the end, both Ramos' and Morata's wives were waiting.

Both players' wives were with their children, so they could not have a greater smile a few hours from the match.
