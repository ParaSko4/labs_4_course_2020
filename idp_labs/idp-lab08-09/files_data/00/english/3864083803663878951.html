Adventure Fitness proudly announces their partnership with Green Beret Challenge to produce obstacle course events nationwide

18 Nov 2019 by admin

Learn how GBC and ADV.FIT can help you set up YOUR OWN CUSTOM EVENT here!

11/15/18 Phoenix, AZ: Adventure Fitness (http://adv.fit), a North Valley AZ-based event company specializing in obstacle course adventures for school fundraisers, corporate team building, and university challenges welcomes longtime friend and associate Mark Ballas's Green Beret Challenge as a partner to expand Adventure Fitness offerings on a national scale.

“Over the past four years, Adventure Fitness has exploded from a few events in Phoenix to challenges of all shapes and sizes across the USA. With Mark’s vast experience in the world of obstacle course racing through creating the wildly successful Green Beret Challenge we’re now able to expand our event production to more locations while increasing the scope of events that we can offer.” says Brett Stewart co-founder of Adventure Fitness.

This announcement comes just days after Adventure Fitness hosted Arizona State University’s “Gold Rush” Family Weekend obstacle course event on-campus on Tempe, AZ and just a short time before their upcoming Ninja Kid’s Camp in Peoria, AZ over Thanksgiving break.

Stewart continued: “For two “OG’s” of the obstacle course racing world to get to pair up like this is fantastic; I wrote the first book on the sport back in 2012 (Ultimate Obstacle Race Training) and Green Beret Challenge was the first challenge of its type in the sport of OCR. Mark’s Special Forces background is an integral part on how he goes about building the types of courses he offers, the challenges he provides for athletes young & old, and the importance of developing teamwork to complete important tasks with important deadlines. For us to bring this hands-on knowledge to schools, universities, and corporate clients is unprecedented!”
Mark, founder and CEO of Green Beret Challenge, stated, “Brett and I have been working together on various OCR championships throughout the past 4 years. We are super stoked to have been selected to partner with Brett & Kristen. It is a rare opportunity when 2 very seasoned RD’s in this very competitive OCR industry are given chance to combine efforts towards a common goal”.

ADV.FIT and GBC’s events are customized for each client; school fundraisers, corporate challenges, and collegiate athletics each have their own branding (ASU’s Gold Rush, NAU’s AXE Factor, MINDBODY’s Ignite, etc.) while retaining the elements of Adventure Fitness’ obstacle courses such as cargo nets, monkey bars, rope walls, Wreck Bags, and adding challenges such as Green Beret Challenge’s sled drag, crawls, climbs, and much more to create a dynamic event to foster team building as well as individual empowerment.

About Adventure Fitness: Based in Phoenix, AZ, Brett and Kristen Stewart created Adventure Fitness to promote healthy, fun, active events at schools around the North Valley. Based on their background in event production with over 12 years experience hosting triathlons, road races, adventure runs, and more, ADV.FIT quickly expanded to cover charity events, corporate team-building challenges, university athletics, and event production for outside companies such as the OCR World Championships, North American OCR Championships, etc. More information can be found online at http://adv.fit

About Green Beret Challenge: Founded in 2012 by former Special Forces, Green Beret, Mark Ballas, the GBC caters to the increasingly growing obstacle course racing (OCR) community and offers participants an array of personal challenges that test strength, physical endurance and mental fortitude. Mark is well-versed in applying military special operations concepts to outdoor enthusiasts, avid athletes and corporate America. He creates a positive atmosphere that engages participants in activities that promote health and fitness; building strength, confidence and self-esteem. Mark’s entrepreneurial spirit and experiences, endured throughout his Special Operations career, ultimately led to the development of the Green Beret Challenge. Mark has also been the Race Director for the 2019 North American OCR Championships and some of his unique obstacles have been featured in the past 3 OCR World Championships.
