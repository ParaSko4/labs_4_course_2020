Black Friday 2019 Latest Wearables Deals: Apple Watch, AirPods

18 Nov 2019 by David Phelan

If you’re after a new smartwatch, Black Friday is the time of year to get involved, for instance with an Apple Watch. Or maybe you want some new wireless in-ears – there are bargains in Apple’s AirPods range, too. Here are the latest deals you should know about.

The deals highlighted within this post were independently selected by the Contributor and do not contain affiliate links.

I’m updating these bargains so please check back. 

Apple Watch

Since its launch in Spring 2015, Apple’s smart timepiece has been ahead of its rivals pretty consistently. The latest model, Apple Watch Series 5, arrived in September this year and is the best yet, with a display that’s always-on, a compass and the new, spiffy design first seen in Fall 2018 when the Apple Watch Series 4 appeared. 

As it’s so new, Series 5 is hard to find at reduced prices, though not impossible and further reductions are expected. Series 4 has been discontinued and, unless the always-on display is what you want, may be the best choice for bargain hunters. After all, it has the same chip as the Series 5 and a near-identical design. 

Then there’s Series 3. First released in Fall 2017, it has a great design, just not quite as gorgeous as the models that followed. Apple recently reduced the price pretty aggressively – it’s now $199 and up – but there are still even better bargains to be had.

Apple Watches come in two sizes. For Series 3, these were 38mm and 42mm, and for Series 4 and 5 there was a slight adjustment to 40mm and 44mm sizes. Choosing the size you prefer is your first task. 

Then, do you want a model with GPS and wi-fi connectivity only or GPS plus cellular, so you can make phone calls direct from your wrist, for instance, even if your phone is at home? Not every model has this option – Series 3 used to but is now only available as wi-fi only.

Your third choice is the look of the Watch. There are several metal finishes to the cases: aluminum, stainless steel and in Series 5 only, titanium or ceramic finishes. Aluminum comes in several shades, as do stainless steel and titanium. 

Oh, and one more thing, there are two special editions: Apple Watch Nike+, which is the same price as a regular Apple Watch, and Apple Watch Hermes, which is noticeably pricier. 

Enough introduction. You can read my review of Apple Watch Series 5 here.
