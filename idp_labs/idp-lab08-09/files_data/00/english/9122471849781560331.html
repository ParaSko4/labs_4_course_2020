Star Rams WR Surprisingly Inactive For Tonight’s Game

18 Nov 2019, 00:10 by Matt Hladik

Earlier: The Los Angeles Rams just released their inactive list for tonight’s game against the Chicago Bears. Surprisingly, Robert Woods was on it.

Woods was not expected to miss tonight’s game and was on the team’s injury report leading up to the game. No reason has been given for his late scratch.

Wide receiver Brandin Cooks (concussion) was already ruled out prior to the game, as was tackle Rob Havenstein (knee) and defensive back Darious Williams (ankle).
