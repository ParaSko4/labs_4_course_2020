Roma's strategy to make Everton Kean to sell

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

For several days, the Everton player Moise Kean has been linked with a move to Roma. The Italians will apparently look for a loan deal with a purchase clause of 22 million euros.

Roma are looking for a striker and Moise Kean, as a result of his problems at Everton, is one of the possible candidates. Everton paid close to 30 million euros for Moise Kean from Juve, but he is not performing well in the Premier League. 

Therefore the Italian could return to Serie A and Roma are interested in getting him for the second half of the season.

'Corriere dello Sport' says that the Italian board's intention is to persuade the English club to reach an agreement this January on his transfer. 

That said, Roma will not sign him straight away. The paper reports that they will take him on loan with a possibility of buying him for 22 million euros.
