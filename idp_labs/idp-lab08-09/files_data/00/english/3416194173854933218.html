Selena Gomez Reacts to Bella Hadid Deleting That Instagram Post

18 Nov 2019, 00:44

Bella Hadid deleted an Instagram post that Selena Gomez commented on, and fans quickly began speculating why.

If you missed it, over the weekend, Bella posted a photo of her posing for a Dior campaign. Selena commented “Stunning” on the photo. Before long, Bella deleted the post without explanation, prompting some speculation about why she took the post away from her Instagram page.

A fan account re-posted the Bella photo and included Selena‘s comment, captioning the photo, “She’s all about supporting women. You all just can’t take it nicely. Btw Ms. Hadid deleted the post.”

Selena saw the post and seemingly reacted to the deletion in her comment, writing, “That sucks,” including a crying emoji.

If you don’t know the backstory, Selena recently re-followed Bella on Instagram, years after they unfollowed each other. They both unfollowed each other after they both dated The Weeknd at different times.
