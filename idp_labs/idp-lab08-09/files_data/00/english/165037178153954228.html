The defender that Guardiola wants at all costs

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Manchester City's defence is plagued by injury problems, and to compensate, the Skyblues will go for a CB in the winter market. The man chosen by Pep Guardiola is the 21-year-old Frenchman Dayot Upamecano, according to 'The Guardian'.

Manchester City won't have problems with money. In fact, they are preparing to spend 115 million euros in January, but 'Kicker' informs that Upamecano's price is now fixed at 60 million.

Upamecano's contract ends in 2021, therefore RB Leipzig wouldn't look on his January sale badly, in order to avoid his value depreciating in the Summer when he only has a year left on his contract. The CB is on the market and Guardiola wants him at all costs.
