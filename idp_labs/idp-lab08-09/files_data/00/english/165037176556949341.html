Cristiano and the peace offering

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

In Italy, they are stating that Cristiano Ronaldo will invite the whole squad for dinner to leave behind any type of grudge after his anger at getting substituted.

Pizza, Pasta and all types of good food can be found in Turin. According to 'La Repubblica', Cristiano Ronaldo intends to have a good time with his Juventus teammates.

Ronaldo himself has explained why he got angry. Between the physical problems he was suffering and his known pride of a champion, Cristiano Ronaldo couldn't hide his anger at getting subbed in Maurizio Sarri's last few matches.

The striker even left the stadium before the Juventus v Milan match ended. This caused some discomfort in the squad. In Italy, they spoke of a meeting between Ronaldo and the board, although the the event wasn't given too much importance.

To iron out the rough edges, Cristiano will invite his teammates to a good restaurant to celebrate the definitive peace after the match against Atalanta, who Juventus play on Saturday. On Wednesday, after playing with Portugal, he will train with his teammates.
