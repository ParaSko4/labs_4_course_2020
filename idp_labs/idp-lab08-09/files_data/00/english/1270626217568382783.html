Woman found guilty of a count of Child Stealing sentenced to 1 year and 10 months imprisonment

18 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

A woman who was found guilty of a count of Child Stealing has been sentenced to 1 year and 10 months imprisonment which has been suspended for two years by the Labasa Magistrates Court.

Asena Raivoli by force or fraud took away a newborn baby from his mother from the Children’s Ward at the Labasa Hospital in April last year.

Magistrate Cama Tuberi says Raivoli left the child in the cassava patch when she realised that police was coming after her.

Magistrate  Tuberi says Raivoli has regretted her action and learnt a lesson from this case.
