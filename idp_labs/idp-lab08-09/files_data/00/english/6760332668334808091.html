It's about who walks with you: Nayanthara - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

18 Nov 2019

'Sye Raa' and 'Whistle' actress Nayanthara is in New York, happily holidaying with her beau Vignesh Shivan, ushering in her birthday that falls today.  The cute couple is enjoying quality time together.

Nayan has posted on her official page an image of her and her soulmate having a relaxed stroll in the famous Central Park.  She captained it, "It's not where you walk, it's who walks with you".

On the work front, Nayanthara's next release is 'Darbar' opposite Superstar Rajinikanth.  Her other upcoming projects are 'Nettrikann', 'Mookuthi Amman' and 'Thala 60' in Tamil.
