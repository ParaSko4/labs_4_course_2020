Boy rapes cousin by tying up her hands & legs - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

18 Nov 2019

A case of rape by a juvenile has come to light in Gurgaon.  The incident happened on Thursday.  A 15-year-old girl was raped by her 16-year-old cousin in the latter's house.

The mother of the victim had asked her daughter to go to her sister-in-law's place to assist her with household chores.  The rapist boy's mother went out for a health check-up.  The boy and his girl cousin were alone at home.

The boy forcibly tied the victim's hands and legs to a cot and committed sexual assault.  The petrified victim didn't open up for the whole day, too shocked as she was.  The next day, when she went to school, the girl fainted.  Since she looked too shocked and depressed, her class teacher probed her.  Upon being asked about her situation repeatedly, the victim talked about the incident.  

Following this, the girl's mother lodged a police complaint.  The perpetrator has been arrested by the police.
