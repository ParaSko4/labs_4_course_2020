NPP Youth Rally Coming To Tamale

18 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The Youth Wing of the New Patriotic Party (NPP) will hold its annual national rally and conference for the year in Tamale in the Northern Region.

The rally is slated for Friday, December 6, 2019, according to an invitation sent out by the National Youth Organizer of NPP, Henry Nana Boakye, aka Nana B.

“Pursuant to the National Council’s approval, the National Youth Wing of the New Patriotic Party invites the general public to the annual National Youth Conference and National Youth Rally in the Northern Regional Capital, Tamale,” the release read.

It said the National Youth Conference would be held in the multi-purpose hall of the University of Development Studies (UDS), Tamale campus, at exactly 9:00 am.

It is to host delegates who are youth, including all ministers and deputy ministers, all regional youth organizers and their deputies, all CEOs, DCEOs and all government appointees, all MMDCEs, all constituency youth organizers and their deputies, TESCON delegation from all accredited institutions, among others.

The National Youth Rally, on the other hand, is slated for Saturday, 7th December 2019 at the Tamale Jubilee Park at exactly 1:00 pm and is open to the general public, it said.
