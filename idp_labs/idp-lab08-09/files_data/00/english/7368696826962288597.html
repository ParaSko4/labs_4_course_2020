Baby Yoda is the Best Thing to Happen to TV in 2019 Thanks to The Mandalorian

18 Nov 2019 by Marco Cocomello

If you have been on the internet in the past 48 hours, you would have already seen a cute little green alien with giant black eyes on your timeline. Baby Yoda has been trending all over the world after he made his debut in the second episode of Disney’s Star Wars: The Mandalorian.

So what is Baby Yoda and why are people losing their minds over its cuteness? Well, if you don’t know, Disney+ launched two weeks ago in the US and along with it the new Star War: The Mandalorian tv show. Unfortunately, here in South Africa, we don’t have Disney+ yet so chances of you knowing who this adorable creature is are quite slim due to the geoblocking restrictions.

Possible episode spoilers to follow

In Chapter 2: The Child, the Mandalorian is tasked with locating and returning a Yoda-looking infant to “the Client”. In fact, this Baby Yoda is actually a 50-year-old creature, which is still a baby because, alien things. This is as much as we know about the creature as we have not watched the episode yet either.

The episode, which you can watch a rundown of all the best Baby Yoda moments down below, consists of some adorable scenes with this ancient-yet-young alien including him swallowing some food in the cutest way possible.
