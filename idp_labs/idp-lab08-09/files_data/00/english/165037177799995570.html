"Lampard is a great guy and could be one of the best coaches in the world"

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Eden Hazard spoke about the quality of his former teammate during his time with the Belgium national time in this international break.

Despite being totally integrated at Real Madrid, Eden Hazard has not forgotten Chelsea. The Belgian was asked when with his national team about his former club and acknowledged that he still thinks about Stamford Bridge.

"I like how they play now, they do it as a united team. I still have friends there and I constantly follow Chelsea. They are winning, so I am happy for them," the Belgian started.

The Belgian praised Chelsea for using their young players and praised his former Chelsea teammate Frank Lampard, who is now the club's coach.

"I'm not surprised at how he's doing. Frank is a great guy, He has shown what he can do and that he can be one of the best coaches in the world in the future," Hazard concluded.
