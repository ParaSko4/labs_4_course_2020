FAKE story captioned ‘Akufo-Addo brings Jet Li to train NPP Militia in karate’ circulating on social media

18 Nov 2019

GhanaWeb has sighted some snapshots of a story with the headline “Akufo-Addo brings Jet Li to train NPP Militia in karate” making rounds on several social media platforms.

With the content of the piece attributed to the former NDC Ashanti Regional Youth Organizer and communicator, Brogya Gyamfi, it was captured with a Ghanaweb template, suggesting that it was published on the website.

Ghanaweb, following this, will like to dissociate itself from the said publication and bring to the fore that it was NEVER at any point published by this website.

The story which has since been denied by Mr. Gyamfi with a rejoinder published on mynewsgh and referenced to www.ghanaweb.com is fake and should be treated with the contempt it deserves.
