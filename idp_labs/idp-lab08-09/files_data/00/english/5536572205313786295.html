Michigan Student Manager Went From Happy To Terribly Afraid Upon Seeing Jim Harbaugh Walk Near Him (VIDEO)

18 Nov 2019, 00:12 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Michigan football sideline was a great place to be on Saturday as the team had no trouble playing their rival, Michigan State, defeating them 44-10 when it was all said and done.

It wasn’t fun for everybody on the Michigan sidelines.

One Michigan student manager could be seen smiling and enjoying life, until he looked up and saw head coach Jim Harbaugh coming his way and he got scared real quick.
