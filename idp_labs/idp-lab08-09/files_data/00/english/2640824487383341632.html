stelios mousarris uses google earth to 3D print a mountain shaped ping pong table

18 Nov 2019 by Mousarris

using images of terrain taken from google earth, cypriot-based designer stelios mousarris has created the ‘plexus ping pong table‘ with a mountain shaped wire base and net. the solid steel structure, which is 3D printed, provides the games table with a stable bottom that will withstand even the strongest ping pong shots, while forming a intriguing and almost sculptural piece.

all images courtesy of stelios mousarris

the ping pong table is designed by mousarris so that it can be easily transformed into a regular table simply by removing the detachable wire print structure that serves as the net. with a span of 274 x 152 x 76 cm, this minimal yet complex design provides plenty of table top surface for you and your guests. a solid walnut timber surface covers the steel 3D printed structure, creating a striking material contrast to the black metal and resulting in a contemporary and unique furniture piece.
