Register for World Climate Summit

18 Nov 2019 by Staff reporter

We are media partners with next month’s World Climate Summit – The Investment COP in Madrid. The COP 25 side event, which will discuss the how of financing climate solutions, takes place on 8 December.

“This year’s Conference of the Parties presents a pivotal opportunity for taking action towards a 1.5 degree world and increasing ambition for Nationally Determined Contributions in advance of next year’s COP in Glasgow. As the leading platform for business- and investment-driven solutions to climate change, World Climate Summit and The Investment COP are committed to offering a dedicated space for catalysing public-private partnerships and cross-sectoral collaboration alongside these negotiations to bring climate goals closer to reality,” said World Climate Ltd CEO Jens Nielsen following the announcement.

The World Climate Summit – The Investment COP is being held at the Madrid Marriott Auditorium Hotel and Conference Center in Madrid. Register now.
