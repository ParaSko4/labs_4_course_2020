United and Arsenal interested in National League youngster

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

He is just 17 years old and he got his first professional contract with Hartlepool United not long back. Arsenal and Man Utd have already got their eyes set on the young goalie.

According to 'The Sun', there is a young talent who is shining in the fifth tier of English football, but they say he could go very far. 

At just 17, he is not playing much for his team at the moment. Hartlepool United gave him a professional contract, his first, to reward him.
