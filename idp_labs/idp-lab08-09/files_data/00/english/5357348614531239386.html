PHOTO: UTECH SCHOLARS

18 Nov 2019

University of Technology (UTech), Jamaica pharmacy students Danielle Dawkins Salmon and Nache Smith were Sunday presented with Vernon and Leila Robinson Scholarship awards following the Grace Allen-Young Memorial Lecture at the university's Papine, St Andrew, campus. Here Dawkins Salmon (second left), who received an award of $200,000, and Smith (second right), who was awarded $100,000, pose with plaques which were presented with their awards. They are joined by Alicia Smith (left), chairman, UTech, Jamaica Pharmacy Alumni; Danielle Robinson (centre), chairperson of the Vernon and Leila Robinson Foundation; and Dr Andrea Daley, head of the School of Pharmacy, UTech. Kevin O'Brien Chang, chairman of Fontana Pharmacies, delivered the Grace Allen-Young Memorial Lecture. (Photo: Joseph Wellington).
