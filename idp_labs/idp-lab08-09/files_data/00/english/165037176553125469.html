Pep's agent denies he is close to Bayern return

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Pep Guardiola's agent, Josep María Orobitg, denied rumours about Pep's possible return to Bayern.

Although neither Manchester City nor Bayern Munich are going through their best spell, it seems complicated that Pep Guardiola is going to leave City to return to the team he was at for 3 years.

The manager's agent, Josep Maria Orobitg, spoke to 'Goal' and 'SPOX' about the rumours of a possible return to Germany.

"I was with Pep on Wednesday and everything's the same. There's a big contract at City for this season and next season", he said.

However, Orobitg didn't want to completely rule out a possible return to Munich. "You never know in football. Pep's good at the team and the city. No one but the press has talked to us about a return".

Guardiola was with Bayern for 3 seasons, from 2013-14 to 2015-16. Since then, he has managed Manchester City, where he has won everything in England but hasn't shone on the European stage.
