Charlotte Crosby and Joshua Ritchie split up after two years together

18 Nov 2019, 00:07

Former Geordie Shore star Charlotte Crosby and boyfriend Joshua Ritchie have broken up after two years together.

The couple decided to part ways as the relationship had ‘broken down’.

Charlotte, 29, announced the news on her Instagram story, posting: ‘Me and Josh have decided to part ways. Sadly the relationship seems to have broken down.

‘Hoping we can still remain civil and wish him the best for the future.’

Josh, however, seems to be doing alright as he has yet to comment but opted for posting clips of him and his pals clubbing instead.

Charlotte only just moved to Bolton to move in with Josh back in August.

She spoke about her troubles of moving out of Newcastle to dating guru Anna Williamson during her stint on Celebs Go Dating.

‘It’s a big deal and sometimes I don’t think he recognises how much a big deal it is,’ the Just Tattoo Of Us star said.

‘I am moving away from everyone I love.’

When asked why she even considered moving in with her beau, Charlotte explained: ‘I want to be with him and I feel excited about starting afresh.’

In October, the reality star even revealed they were happier than ever since taking the plunge.

She had said: ‘We’re really happy! It’s amazing, it’s never been better, and I’m not just saying that. The love that I have for that boy is just overwhelmingly strong. It could bring us to tears, that’s how much I love him.

‘How is everything so perfect? I’ve just got this overwhelming feeling, it’s better than better, you can’t even top it. I’m happier than happy, it’s euphoric!’

Well, that aged badly.

The two were inseparable after getting together in February 2018 but have split right before their second anniversary.
