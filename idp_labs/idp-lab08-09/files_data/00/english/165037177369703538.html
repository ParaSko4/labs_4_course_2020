PSG, Barca and RM fighting for Fabian and Skriniar

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Fabian Ruiz and Milan Skriniar are wanted by three clubs. Barca, Real Madrid and Paris Saint-Germain.

In every transfer window, certain names become the most talked about when it comes to signings. Fabian Ruiz and Milan Skriniar are two of those. 

According to the latest news, they will also be talked about for the next window. This Monday, it was confirmed that Fabian has put off his new contract as he is waiting for Barca. But the Catalans are not the only ones interested.

Real Madrid were linked with the Napoli man on numerous occasions. 'Los Blancos' are still looking for talented players who have yet to hit their top level. However, Fabian's decision to hold off on his new contract is a big boost for them.

So much so that PSG, according to 'Mundo Deportivo', have also got in the fight. He is not the only players the three teams want. Milan Skriniar is also on their list. 

The Inter Milan central defender turned down Barca in the past and Real Madrid are interested in him. However, 'TMW' reports PSG are also keen on him. 

It will be interesting to see where the futures of these two players lie.
