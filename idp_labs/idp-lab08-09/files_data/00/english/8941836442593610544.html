Su’a Cravens safe after alarming social media posts

18 Nov 2019 by Matthew Paras

Su’a Cravens is safe after the former Redskins safety posted an alarming series of social media posts, according to a pair of former teammates. 

Will Blackmon, who played with Cravens in 2016, gave an update on Cravens early Monday morning. Linebacker Will Compton also seemingly shared an update on Cravens as well, saying “All is okay. No need to worry anymore. He is safe and with family.”

Late Sunday, Cravens posted a number of messages that caused people to worry, including one that said, “If these are my last days, I want to say I love you and I always will. God I leave the rest up to you” and another that said “Goodbye” and included a heart emoji. Cravens then wiped the content on all his social media accounts.
