Time for a jar?

18 Nov 2019, 00:01

Sir, – The blurbs produced by wine merchants these days grow ever more bizarre. One prominent retailer is promoting a wine at the moment on the basis that it is “bursting with strawberry jam flavours”.

Hands up all those who like their wine to taste like strawberry jam! – Yours, etc,

Co Dublin.
