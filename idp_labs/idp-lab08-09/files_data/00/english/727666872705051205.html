Rapper Succeed Phlyguy Murdered Through Pizza Delivery Scam

18 Nov 2019, 00:28 by AllHipHop Staff

The cops say a man in Henderson, Nevada hatched a scheme to rob rapper Succeed Phlyguy using pizza delivery scheme.

(AllHipHop News) Police in Henderson, Nevada have finally apprehended a man involved in the senseless killing of rapper Succeed Phlyguy.

Succeed Phlyguy, real name, Ronnie Cravens Jr., was shot and killed during a home invasion on October 12th.

According to the cops, a man named Angell Cordero Fernandez surprised the rapper by obtaining access through his home's garage and simply ringing the doorbell.

Once someone answered the door, Fernandez barged inside and attempted to rob Succeed Phlyguy and his roommates.

A shootout followed, and Succeed Phlyguy was hit and killed in the crossfire.

But investigators say the home invasion was well-planned out over the course of several days.

Fernandez used a dummy number to text message the rapper with offers of free pizzas two different times in the days leading up to the murder.

Surveillance footage captured the home invasion and Fernandez made the blunder of momentarily exposing his face during the robbery.

Fernandez managed to scurry away with Succeed Phlyguy's diamond chain and his Rolex watch.

According to News3 Las Vegas, the getaway car Fernandez drove to commit the home invasion/murder was easily traceable, because it was registered to his wife.

Angell Cordero Fernandez is facing a litany of charges, including Conspiracy to Commit Robbery, Attempted Murder and Open Murder.
