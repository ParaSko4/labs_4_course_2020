Alex Marquez announced at Repsol Honda for 2020

2019 Moto2™ World Champion will partner brother and eight-time Champion Marc Marquez in MotoGP™ next season

18 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Honda Racing Corporation have announced the signing of double World Champion Alex Marquez. The young Spanish rider and reigning Moto2™ Champion will join the Repsol Honda Team on a one-year contract.
