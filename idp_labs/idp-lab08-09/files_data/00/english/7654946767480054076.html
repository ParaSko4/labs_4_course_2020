Bengals’ Auden Tate carried off on stretcher after scary hit

18 Nov 2019, 00:36 by Post Sports Desk

Bengals wide receiver Auden Tate gave fans a thumbs up as he was taken off the field on a stretcher following a scary hit Sunday in Oakland.

Tate laid motionless on his back after getting tackled in the head area by Raiders’ safety Curtis Riley, but appeared to have full movement in his extremities while being carried off.

Riley was not penalized on the play.
