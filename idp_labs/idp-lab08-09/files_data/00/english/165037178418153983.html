Ibra closer to joining Bologna than Milan

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

In Italy, they are assuring that the Swedish striker Zlatan Ibrahimovic is much closer to signing for Bologna to lend a hand to his friend Sinisa Mihajlovic.

It seems that things are becoming clearer and Zlatan Ibrahimovic's future will once again lie in Italy. The striker will thus return to the country where he played the best in his career and where he will be welcomed with open arms.

Although it seemed like the option of Milan was most probable, in the last few hours 'La Gazzetta dell Sport' has stated the Bologna move is more possible.

According to the aforementioned source, Zlatan would prefer to join Bologna. He would join to help his friend Sinisa Mihajlovic, who remains in charge of the club despite his serious illness

According to Bologna's sporting director, Ibrahimovic and Mihajlovic have already spoken by phone a few times and in the last few hours, Marco Di Vaio has flown to Los Angeles to offer Zlatan a deal.

In any case, despite the Bologna offer seeming closer, the Milan return hasn't been ruled out. Zlatan's wife, Helena, wants to live there again and Ibra wouldn't dislike helping a team he loves either and that have been having a disastrous spell the last few years. Everything will be clear in a matter of weeks.
