Why I Love Sharing Nude Pictures Of Myself – Actress, Efia Odo

18 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Efia Odo Speaks On Her Dress Style

Popular Ghanaian actress and media personality, Efia Odo has revealed why her sultry pictures are always found on the social media space.

The actress, however, maintained that she doesn’t go nude but rather dresses half-naked.

Explaining why she loves dressing half-naked, Odo said she knows she has a beautiful body and she loves seeing herself half-naked.

“I don’t get nude. I get half nude and it’s just how I am. I wake up in the morning, if I wanna be fully covered, I’ll be fully covered. If I wanna be half-naked, I can wear a bikini down the street if I want to but I’m in Ghana so I can’t,” she told Pulse Chat.

The actress added that she believes going half-naked pleases her fans and boosts her brand as well.

“For my brand, I think it kinda does pay off because people love seeing me half-naked and I love seeing myself half-naked.

“I think if I was fat I wouldn’t be half-naked but I got a pretty good body so why not show it off,” the actress added.
