Egypt faces Comoros in search for hard three points

18 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

The technical meeting of the match between Egypt and Comoros resulted in the appearance of the Pharaohs in traditional colors; red & white.

Egypt are preparing to face the Comoros on Monday at 03:00 p.m. in the second round of the African 2021 qualifiers in Cameroon.

The technical meeting was held to face the Comoros and Egypt in the presence of the referees and the observers of the game, the representative of Comoros and Mohamed Barakat, the national team director.

During the meeting, it was agreed on the details of the game where the Egyptian team in traditional costume in the colors of the Egyptian flag, while the Comoros team will show in full green.

The national team striker, Ahmed Gomaa, missed the team journey to Comoros as he suffered an injury on Friday training session.

The Pharaohs aim to bounce back after a poor start at the Qualifiers on Thursday night in Alexandria as Egypt ended the Qualifiers opener with a 1-1 draw with Kenya.

On the other hand, Comoros secured an away victory over Togo thanks to a goal from the nation’s biggest star, Faïz Selemani.

Comoros lead the group with three points, two points ahead of Egypt and Kenya share the second position while Togo stays at the bottom with zero points.
