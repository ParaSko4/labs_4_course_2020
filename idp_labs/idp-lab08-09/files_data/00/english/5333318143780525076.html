Look who’s talking sustainability…

18 Nov 2019 by Staff reporter

A top line-up of inspirational speakers will take to the stage next month in Madrid for the 10th anniversary Sustainable Innovation Forum 2019 (SIF19). Renowned for bringing together public and private sector stakeholders every year on a mission to accelerate the sustainability agenda, it is a leading COP 25 side event.

Mary Robinson, the hugely respected former President of Ireland; Maimunah Mohd Sharif, Executive Director, United Nations Human
Settlements Programme; and Isabella Lövin, Deputy Prime Minister & Minister for Environment & Climate, Sweden, are among the speakers.

The forum will be held at Novotel Madrid Campo de las Naciones on 10 and 11 December. We are media partners with the event, so readers can get a 15% discount: simply add the code InnovatorsMag15 at checkout to redeem this special delegate pass.
