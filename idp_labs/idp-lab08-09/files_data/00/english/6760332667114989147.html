Yesterday, Ungal Naan, a grand event to celebrate Kamal Haasan's 65th birthday as well as his 60th year in films was held in Nehru Indoor Stadium, and Superstar Rajnikanth, the best friend of Kamal for more than four decades was present throughout the event, and gave a stunning speech.

During his speech, he said "It is impossible for anyone in cinema to display multiple talents like Kamal Haasan. 60 years of journey isn't an ordinary thing, he has faced lots of tough times and sacrificed a lot. As a conductor even I faced tough times but they are nothing compared to his.I have always felt happy seeing Kamal Haasan's acting".
