Volunteer Day at St Elizabeth Infirmary

18 Nov 2019

Lajean Powell-Richards (right), parish coordinator for disaster preparedness in the Manchester Municipal Corporation, and inspector of poor for Manchester, Densil Mundy (centre) are assisted by Sandals South Coast landscaper Keneil Campbell in planting hibiscus inside a therapeutic garden during the recent Volunteer Day activities at the St Elizabeth Infirmary.

A St Elizabeth Infirmary resident gets a neck and back massage from Sasholey Stolz of Sandals South Coast Red Lane Spa, while Spa Manager Donnette Armstrong (second right) engages Errol Lebert (right), CEO of the St Elizabeth Municipal Corporation and Catherine Williams (left), acting inspector of poor for St Elizabeth. Occasion was Volunteer Day activities at the infirmary recently.
