Romeo Beckham and Mia Moocher growing closer as they take in ATP Tennis final

They are rumoured to be dating and tongues will be wagging again as the teenagers take in Stefanos Tsitsipas' final success against Dominic Thiem at The O2 in London

18 Nov 2019, 00:44 by Sam Elliott, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sam-elliott/

Smiling Romeo Beckham and Mia Moocher seems to be growing even closer.

The 17-year-old - son of England great David Beckham - has been sparking romance rumours with the young model for weeks.

She accompanied him to a family dinner as they toasted his mum Victoria's recent fashion show success.

Now they have stepped out together again as they enjoyed a night at the tennis.

The were in London to watch the climax of the ATP finals at The O2 arena.

 

They watched Stefanos Tsitsipas beat Austrian Dominic Thiem in the men's singles final match - and appeared to be getting cosy.

Smiling and giggling throughout the first set, they were in a relaxed mood during the last tennis tournament of the year.

They have yet to confirm if they are officially an item.

But Romeo has already made an appearance on Mia's Instagram stories as they continue to enjoy each other's company.

 

He isn't the only Beckham boy being linked with a bit of romance.

Brother Brooklyn is not hanging about when it comes to finding a new beau as he has been linked with his fourth woman in as many months as he looks for 'the one'.

He has gone 'official' with 24-year-old Marvel actress Nicola Peltz.

"Brooklyn has already met her family and he gets on very well with her brother, they even partied together," a source told The Sun.

"He is becoming quite prolific in his wooing. He has obviously inherited his dad’s charm.

"He’s not naturally the most ­gregarious of boys, but his success rate with girls is quite astonishing. His pals egg him on but David and Victoria are less impressed."
