"I am already prepared"

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

After more than a month without playing for Real Madrid, Bale did so with Wales. The attacker has stated in the press conference before the game against Hungary, that he is already ready to have more minutes.

Gareth Bale has been under the spotlight for weeks because nobody knew why he was out for so long. It was due to an injury, but Madrid ddi not include this in their medical statements and Zidane did not know how to respond to this case.

The French coach only said that the player wasn't available, something that Bale had already proved wrong after playing against Azerbaijan for an hour.

"I have not played for four weeks and for this it was important to have this hour against Azerbaijan. I am ready", he said in the press conference before Wales' game against Hungary.
