Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Perfume Sets For Women

18 Nov 2019 by Tiffany Leigh

Coco Chanel once said that “no elegance is possible without perfume. It is the unseen, unforgettable, ultimate accessory.” In essence, it is an expression of your personality without you needing to utter a word; it’s also definitely one lively and lingering way to command the attention of a room. So in light of the upcoming holiday season, if you’re on the hunt for that perfect gift (for yourself or that special someone) - what better way to spice up the season than with these scent sets that are sure to be intoxicating winners.These are the Best Perfume Sets For Women to celebrate all things festive and fragrant:

Chanel Discovery Set

This limited edition collection is for the person seeking a daily “scentspiration”. 15 vials (at 0.12-ounce each) offer a range of unique scents and capture the spirit of Gabrielle Chanel. Each fragrance is named after a story or memory in her life (e.g. Bois Des Iles, Jersey, 1932). And ‘31 Rue Cambon’ is one to note as well, where elegance and warmth is captured in this fragrance - thanks to a mixture of bergamont, patchouli and citrus. The perfume’s name is in honor of a real address in Paris - which not only acted as the very first and flagship Chanel store, but was also the site of Chanel’s famous apartment (located on the second-floor).

Chanel Coromandel and Maisons D’Art Coffret

If money is no object, then this collector’s fragrance is for you. At $10,000 USD and only 9 pieces available in the country, it’s the finest expression of beauty and grace. The coffret (box) is crafted by Maison Lesage, embroiderer since 1858 - whose elegant tweed lines the interior, meant to mimic those famous Chinese-lacquered screens. Nestled in the center is the Coromandel perfume - crafted by Chanel’s in-house Perfumer Creator Olivier Polge, and housed in a collectible Baccarat crystal bottle. The wearer of this fragrance will be embraced by what Polge describes as an “Oriental scent” with an amber intensity, that’s fortified with warm musky notes. To finish, draped over the bottle are handcrafted gems by Maison Goossens, goldsmith-jewelers since 1950. Straddling the realms of art and beauty, this is a must-have item for any perfume aficionado.

Jo Malone London Travel Size Cologne Set

This bespoke gift is ideal for the worldly jetsetter, and yes - you can tote one or two of these miniature fragrances in your carry-on baggage. With 5 different scents in this set, each one is a perfect expression to wear on its own or for layering to suit your mood and/or occasion. Comes with 5 x 0.3-ounce of the following: Lime Basil & Mandarin Cologne; English Pear & Freesia Cologne; Honeysuckle & Davana Cologne; Peony & Blush Suede Cologne; Wood Sage & Sea Salt Cologne.

Tom Ford Private Blend F*cking Fabulous Deluxe Gift

The perfect gift for that bold and vivacious person, this scent is described as an oriental blend of earthy clary sage and lavender. Meanwhile, almond and vanilla add nutty sweetness, with undertones of leather and tonkabean. Fine blonde woods lends a creamy finish to this decidedly memorable fragrance. The gift set comes with an Eau de Parfum Decanter, Candle, and Atomizer with Funnel.

Le Labo Discovery Set

For the one who wants a spectrum of creationist and bespoke scents, Le Labo has got you covered. Their ultimate Discovery Set allows you to have their entire collection- all 17 scents in 0.05-ounce vials. And when you’ve smelled your way to “the one”, also included in this gift is a redeemable card which allows you to choose one of their fragrances in a larger 3.4-ounce Eau de Parfum format.

Kilian Good Girl Gone Bad Holiday Gift Set

Whether naughty or nice, it’s all sugar and spice with this festive gift. In this holiday set you receive a duo of treats, styled appropriately in a Christmas themed coffret; it includes a 1.7-ounce refillable perfume and 6.8-ounce body lotion from their Body Pleasures Collection. The wearer of this fragrance will be draped in sweet fruits and florals which include apricot-tinged osmanthus, orange blossom and rose. A trifecta of flowers unite to lend a unique finish to the fragrance: tuberose absolute, jasmine and narcissus.

Bond No. 9 Swarovski Triple Decker

Seeking to bring glitz, glam, and a major wow-factor to your gift giving this season? Then the $16,000 USD Swarovski Triple Decker Parfum set is for you. Crafted by Bond No. 9 - this bespoke set comes with 9 of their highly sought-after scents. Each 3.4-ounce bottle is decked out in Swarovski crystals and nestled in a keepsake velvet box (which is also adorned in Swarovski stones). Scents in this range pay particular homages to NYC and include Park Avenue South, New York Musk, Perfumista Avenue, Madison Square Park, Queens, B9, The Scent of Peace, Madison Avenue and Bond No 9 Signature Perfume.

Viktor&Rolf Flowerbomb Eau de Parfum Set

This charming snowflake-shaped gift offers 4 ways to wear Flowerbomb: 2x Eau de Parfum (in 1.7-ounce and 0.67-ounce), Body Lotion, and an Eau de Parfum Travel Spray (0.25-ounce). For those who desire an ultra feminine fragrance, the wearer will be enveloped in sensual tones whose top notes include bergamot and mandarin; middle notes of jasmine and rose; and a base that include patchouli, praline, amber and vanilla. With fresh and floral aromas, this set is sure to bring a welcome burst of sunny sentiments, even on the coldest days of winter.
