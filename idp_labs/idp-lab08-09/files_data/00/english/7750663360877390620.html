Arsenal sound out Luis Enrique over replacing Unai Emery?

Arsenal have reportedly sounded out the possibility of installing Luis Enrique as manager following the club's recent poor run of form under Unai Emery.

18 Nov 2019, 00:19 by Leo Nieboer

Emery finds himself under pressure after managing just one win from his last six games - a spell that has left the Gunners sixth in the Premier League table.

It is claimed that the Spaniard has one month - or six games - to save his position at the Emirates.

According to Football London, Arsenal are looking at Enrique as a potential candidate for the role should results fail to improve under the current regime.

Enrique enjoyed a successful spell as Barcelona manager between 2014 and 2017, winning the treble during his first season at the Camp Nou.

The 49-year-old, who took over the Spain job after the 2018 World Cup, has been out of work since July in order to spend more time with his family.
