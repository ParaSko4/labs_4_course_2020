Ant McPartlin baffles I'm A Celeb fans with brand new tattoos

Ant McPartlin was back on tonight's show after a year off and it looks like he has a new look too

18 Nov 2019 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin has stunned I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! viewers as he displayed several new tattoos on his arms.

The telly host, 43, debuted his new inking in a rolled-up short sleeved shirt as his toned arms were almost popping out.

Standing next to co-host Dec Donnelly, 44, the presenter folded his arms and gave fans a good look at his tats.

Baffled fans were trying to work out if the star had always had the tattoos of an arrow and a rose which were neatly outlined on his forearms.
