Chelsea add themselves to the list of targets for Reinier

18 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Reinier Carvalho is one of the most sought-after footballers in the Brazilian league. There are a lot of clubs that want the Flamengo attacker, such as Barcelona, Atletico and PSG. And, according to 'The Sun', Chelsea are the latest team to join this list.

Reinier Carvalho's breakthrough in Brazilian football has caught the attention of many European giants. The 17-year-old attacker is impressing at Flamengo and the rumours keep on coming.

Clubs such as Atletico and Barcelona are interested in the youngster, recently joined by Paris-Saint-Germain. Now, 'The Sun' states that Chelsea have also entered onto the list of suitors for Reinier.
