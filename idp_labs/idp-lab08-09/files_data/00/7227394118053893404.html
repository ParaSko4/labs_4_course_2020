European pre-orders for the Motorola Razr 2019 start next month, here's the price

18 Nov 2019 by Peter

Europeans are looking at a €1,600 price tag for the Motorola Razr 2019 – slightly less than a leaked report suggested. However, Motorola leadership didn’t offer more details, saying only that “select markets” will get it. Here’s what we know so far.

British carrier EE has claimed exclusivity on the phone in the UK. Pre-orders will start in December, but the phone won’t ship until January. The SIM-free price in pounds is still TBD (it should be around £1,400).

Motorola Razr 2019 pre-orders in Italy are set to start on December 4, sales will likely kick off in January again. We haven’t heart anything about carrier exclusives yet.

Judging by rumors, Germany, Sweden and the Netherlands will join the party too. The Swedish launch in particular is reportedly set for January 24.
