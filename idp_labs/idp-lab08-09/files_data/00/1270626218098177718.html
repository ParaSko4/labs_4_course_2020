PM urges families to get themselves vaccinated against measles

18 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

Prime Minister Voreqe Bainimarama is urging Fijian families to get themselves vaccinated against measles.

While speaking in parliament today, Bainimarama says if anyone has any doubt, they should visit the nearest health centres to have their child's health records checked, they will be immunized on the spot if needed.
