India makes advance payment for Russian missile system: Rostec CEO Sergey Chemezov

Chemezov said negotiations were underway and a contract with India will be completed in 2025.

18 Nov 2019 by Reuters

India has made an advance payment of $800 million for Russia's S-400 anti-aircraft missile systems, the head of Russian conglomerate Rostec Sergey Chemezov told reporters on Monday.
