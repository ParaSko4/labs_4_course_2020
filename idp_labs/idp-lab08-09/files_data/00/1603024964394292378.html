Jubilant Life Sciences promoter sells Rs 57 crore worth shares

Promoter entity sold shares at a price of Rs 493 per share, as per bulk deals data available on the BSE website November 18.

18 Nov 2019

Promoter of Jubilant Life Sciences reduced its stake in the pharma firm on November 18 by selling 11.6 lakh equity shares of the company.

Jubilant Stock Holding Private Limited sold shares at a price of Rs 493 per share, bulk deal data available with BSE showed.

The deal is estimated to be worth Rs 57.19 crore.

Close

Promoter and promoter group of Jubilant Life Sciences held 50.68 per cent stake as of September 30, of which, Jubilant Stock Holding owned 14.14 per cent.

The stock closed at Rs 494.50, up Rs 2.20, or 0.45 percent on BSE.

Among other bulk deals, Axis Trustee Services Limited sold 8,75,23,261 shares of Reliance Communications at Rs 0.58 per share and Vistra ITCL (India) offloaded 43,17,515 shares of Sintex Plastics Tech at Rs 1.22 per share.
