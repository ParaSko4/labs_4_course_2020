19-Yr-Old Rapper Tay-K Currently Serving 55 Years In Prison Indicted For Second Murder

18 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Rapper Tay-K has been indicted on a capital murder charge in Bexar County, Texas, as San Antonio ABC affiliate KSAT News reports. The charge stems from a 2017 incident, in which Tay-K allegedly shot and killed a 23-year-old photographer named Mark Anthony Saldivar during a robbery.
The incident occurred on April 23, 2017, when Tay-K, whose real name is Taymor Travon McIntyre, allegedly shot Saldivar after stealing the photographer’s equipment in San Antonio. Tay-K was on the lam at the time of the shooting, fleeing house arrest after he was apprehended for allegedly participating in a botched robbery that left one man dead in 2016.
