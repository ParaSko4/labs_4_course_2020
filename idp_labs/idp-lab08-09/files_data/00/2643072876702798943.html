Mikel Describes Eden Hazard As A Lazy Player

18 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

Former Nigeria captain, John Mikel Obi has described Eden Hazard as a lazy player but who always gets the job done.

Speaking on the sideline of a football event in an interview with beIN SPORTS, the ex-Nigerian international also berated the Belgian captain who won the Champions League, the Europa League, the Premier League twice and three FA Cups with him during his 11 years at Chelsea.

Mikel who also praised the skillful player said Hazard has an incredible talent, maybe not as good as [Lionel] Messi, but he can do whatever he wants with the ball at his feet.

“He didn’t like to train hard. While we were working he was waiting for us to finish training just standing there. But on Sundays he was always man of the match, it was unbelievable.”
