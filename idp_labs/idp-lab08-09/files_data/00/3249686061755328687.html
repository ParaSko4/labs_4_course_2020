SuperM to donate proceeds from 'Let's Go Everywhere' to the Global Poverty Project

18 Nov 2019, 00:42 by jennywill

SuperM will be donating proceeds from 'Let's Go Everywhere' to the Global Poverty Project.

The song, a collaboration with Korean Air, will be released later tonight at 6PM KST on the 18th. The song is an electro pop dance song by Kenzie, and the MV has been airing at the beginning of all Korean Air flights.

Proceeds from the song will be donated to the Global Citizen campaign run by the Global Poverty Project. Make sure to check it out when it's out later tonight.
